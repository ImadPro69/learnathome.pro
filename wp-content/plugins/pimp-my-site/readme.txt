== Description ==
Add some cool stickers, sprinkle some particles on top, Pimp your Site and make it shiny for every occasion!

= Ready in one click =
Ready-made effects presets. In one click, your site is pimped!

= Customize your designs =
Use a preset, customize it, or start from scratch and make your own selection of elements. You are in control!

= Trendy site all year long =
New presets will keep coming to keep your site up to date with the current festivities, trends, and more.
